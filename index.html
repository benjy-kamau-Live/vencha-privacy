Privacy Policy for Vencha

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

TL;DR: We respect your privacy. We collect only what's necessary to provide the Vencha experience, never sell your data, and give you full control over your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vencha ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture, and bio (optional)
  • User-Generated Content: Challenges you create, participate in, comments, likes, bookmarks, and reactions
  • Media Content: Photos and videos you upload for challenges

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, challenges viewed, interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs for app improvement
  • Location Data: Approximate location (if you grant permission) for location-based challenges

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Vencha's services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable you to participate in challenges and interact with other users
  • Personalize your experience and provide relevant content
  • Send you important updates, notifications, and administrative messages
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and features
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Encrypted password storage (bcrypt hashing)
  • Row-level security policies on database
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure authentication tokens stored in device secure storage

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 We Share Data Only When:

  • With Other Users: Your public profile, challenges, and interactions are visible to other Vencha users
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and Expo for app infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you'll be notified)
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing will require your explicit permission

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you
  • Correct Your Data: Update inaccurate or incomplete information through your profile settings
  • Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions for location, camera, or media access at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Object to Processing: Object to specific uses of your data

7. Children's Privacy

Vencha is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete the information.

8. Third-Party Services

Vencha uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo: App infrastructure and updates (Privacy Policy)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Public content (challenges, comments) may be retained for app functionality but anonymized
  • Backup copies are deleted according to our backup retention schedule (90 days)

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Vencha uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored securely on your device to keep you logged in
  • App Preferences: Local storage of your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending you an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of Vencha after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Your Consent

By using Vencha, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@vencha.app
App: Use "Help & Support" in the Profile section
Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

Additional Information for Specific Regions

For European Economic Area (EEA) Users (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: For optional features like location-based challenges
  • Contract Performance: To provide the Vencha service
  • Legitimate Interests: For app improvement and security

You have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

For California Users (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights
