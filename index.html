Privacy Policy for Vencha

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

For Kenyan Users: We are committed to protecting your data in compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019. We collect only what's necessary to provide the Vencha experience, never sell your data, and give you full control over your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vencha ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Compliance with Kenyan Law: Vencha operates in compliance with the Data Protection Act, 2019 and regulations issued by the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) of Kenya. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of all our users, particularly those in Kenya.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture, and bio (optional)
  • User-Generated Content: Challenges you create, participate in, comments, likes, bookmarks, and reactions
  • Media Content: Photos and videos you upload for challenges

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, challenges viewed, interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs for app improvement
  • Location Data: Approximate location (if you grant permission) for location-based challenges

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Vencha's services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable you to participate in challenges and interact with other users
  • Personalize your experience and provide relevant content
  • Send you important updates, notifications, and administrative messages
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and features
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Data Storage and Security

Data Storage Location: Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on Google Cloud Platform - GCP) with servers located in secure data centers. While our infrastructure is cloud-based, we ensure compliance with Kenyan data protection requirements.

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL) - all data sent between your device and our servers is encrypted
  • Encrypted password storage (bcrypt hashing) - your passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Row-level security policies on database - ensuring users can only access their own data
  • Regular security audits and updates to protect against vulnerabilities
  • Secure authentication tokens stored in device secure storage (not accessible to other apps)
  • Firewall protection and intrusion detection systems
  • Access controls limiting who can view or modify data

Data Protection Act Compliance: In accordance with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019, we have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 We Share Data Only When:

  • With Other Users: Your public profile, challenges, and interactions are visible to other Vencha users
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and Expo for app infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you'll be notified)
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing will require your explicit permission

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and obtain a copy of it
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data through your profile settings
  • Right to Deletion (Right to be Forgotten): Request deletion of your personal data when it's no longer necessary for the purposes collected
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your personal data for specific purposes
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request restriction on how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions for location, camera, or media access at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner if you believe your rights have been violated

How to Exercise Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vencha.app or through the "Help & Support" section in the app. We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by the Data Protection Act.

7. Children's Privacy

Vencha is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete the information.

8. Third-Party Services

Vencha uses the following third-party services to provide our platform:

  • Supabase (hosted on Google Cloud Platform): Database hosting, authentication, and storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP): Cloud infrastructure services (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo: Mobile app infrastructure and over-the-air updates (Privacy Policy)

Data Processing Agreements: We have ensured that our third-party service providers comply with applicable data protection laws and have appropriate security measures in place to protect your data.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Public content (challenges, comments) may be retained for app functionality but anonymized
  • Backup copies are deleted according to our backup retention schedule (90 days)

10. International Data Transfers

For Kenyan Users: Your data is primarily processed using cloud infrastructure (Supabase/GCP), which may involve data being stored on servers outside Kenya. We ensure that:

  • All international data transfers comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019
  • Adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data as required by Section 48 of the Act
  • Our service providers (Supabase and Google Cloud Platform) maintain internationally recognized security certifications
  • Your data enjoys the same level of protection regardless of where it's physically stored

Legal Basis for Transfer: Data transfers are necessary for the performance of our contract with you (providing the Vencha service) and are protected by appropriate technical and organizational measures.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Vencha uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored securely on your device to keep you logged in
  • App Preferences: Local storage of your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
