⚠️ Important Notice: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Vencha account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our privacy team using the button below or directly to privacy@venchaapp.com
  2. Include in your email:
    • The email address associated with your Vencha account
    • Your display name or username (if different from email)
    • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. We will verify your identity and confirm the deletion request within 48 hours
  4. Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days of confirmation

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your Vencha account, the following data will be permanently removed:

Data Type What Happens Timeline
Account Information Email, password, name - permanently deleted Within 30 days
Profile Data Display name, bio, profile picture - permanently deleted Within 30 days
Personal Preferences Settings, notifications, bookmarks - permanently deleted Within 30 days
Private Messages All private communications - permanently deleted Within 30 days
Authentication Data Login tokens, session data - immediately invalidated Immediate

What Might Be Retained (Anonymized)

In accordance with our Privacy Policy, some content may be retained for app functionality but will be completely anonymized:

  • Public Challenges: Challenges you created may remain visible but will show as "Deleted User" with no link to your identity
  • Comments and Reactions: Your public interactions may remain visible but anonymized (no profile picture, name, or identifying information)
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics used for app improvement
Why retain anonymized content? This ensures that other users' experiences and interactions remain intact. For example, if you participated in a challenge, other users' comments and context remain meaningful.

Backup Retention Period

Your data is removed from our active systems within 30 days. However, backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days according to our backup retention schedule. These backups are:

  • Stored securely and not accessible for any purpose
  • Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Used only for disaster recovery purposes

Alternative: Temporary Account Deactivation

If you're unsure about permanent deletion, you can temporarily deactivate your account by:

  1. Opening the Vencha app
  2. Going to Profile → Settings
  3. Selecting "Deactivate Account" (if available)

Deactivation hides your profile and content without deleting it. You can reactivate anytime by logging back in.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct your data: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your data: Request permanent deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Port your data: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing: Stop certain types of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@venchaapp.com or our Data Protection Officer at dpo@venchaapp.com
